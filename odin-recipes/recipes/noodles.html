Perfect 2-Minute Noodles

Description

A quick snack that is easy to make but a skill to make well

Incredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Flavouring

Instructions

  1. Place the block in a pot and fill with water to half the height of the block
  2. Add a third of the flavouring packet to the pot and close the lid
  3. When the water starts to boil, remove the lid, turn over the block and add a second third of the packet
  4. When the water is almost gone, start stirring rigorously and add the last of the flavouring
