Sushi Rice

Description

Delicious sticky sushi rice that's way easier than you would think to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice untill water runs clear
  2. Combine with water and bring to a boil, turn down heat to low
  3. Combine other ingedients in a small saucepan
  4. Let the rice and the sue cool
  5. Mix the sue into the rice
